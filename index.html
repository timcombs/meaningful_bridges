About My Practice

I am a licensed therapist with over a decade of experience helping people navigate life's challenges. My approach is holistic and client-centered, focusing on creating a safe and supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings without judgment. I specialize in anxiety, depression, and relationship issues, using evidence-based practices to help you build resilience and achieve lasting change.

Reconnect, Repair, Rediscover

My goal is to empower you with the tools and insights you need to live a more fulfilling life. I believe that every individual has the capacity for growth and healing, and I am here to guide you on that path.
